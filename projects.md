## WebApps for your 🐳 homelab

If you are a "homlaber", you love running your own servers and services. I have built a few apps to help me scratch my own itch. You can find some here how to install and use them on your homelab.

### LabcastARR (open source version comming soon)

A podcast channel for your Homelab. This app will let you create a podcast channel for your Homelab by creating episodes from YouTube videos or uploading your own audio files


### 2watchARR (open source version comming soon)

Your own playlists & "watch later" list for your Homelab. This app will let you create a your own playlists for Youtube and Podcasts in your Homelab to better manage what and when you watch or listen to media.
